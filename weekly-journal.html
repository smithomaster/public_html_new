2nd December 2018

T – 5 Weeks

Today, with the new arrival of the 3d printer, a team has been working on building the printer. Declan, Byron and James are putting together our 3d printer, which can print with a carbon fibre infused nylon material, which is very strong. Declan says “It’s pretty cool”. We would like to thank our sponsors who made this possible.

The media team is sending out emails to local business offering the opportunity to purchase squares on our “Friends of Koalafied” banner. They are also planning a new website design.

Piotr and Max are working on our team’s selection for the FIRST Choice. They have a set amount of credits that they can use towards this, and they are prioritising what they will put the team’s credits towards in this.

Jacob and Jem are making a frame to support the shooter which the team is building, and what they say on the topic is “She’ll be right”.


18th November 2018

T - 7 Weeks

This week the mechanical team has focused its efforts on designing the Basketball-Shooter’s intake. The Media team has focused on networking with Pembroke Partners to fill vacant spaces in the Friends of Koalafied Banner.

The media team is working on new shirt designs to represent the 2019 sponsors. Finally the media team are working on a database that will collate the availability of members during the 2019 season. The Software team has made progress on the pathfinder code and vision code has been further developed.


11th November 2018

T - 8 Weeks

Today the team has been working on the preparations and plans to go to Sydney with further discussions being held later. The build team has been working on more work with the robot interior such as, the welding of metal and bending the pipes. Meanwhile the software team were working on the coding for the robot’s motor. The team has also had a successful open day and gained potential members from the last two weeks.


28th October 2018

T - 10 Weeks

Today the Build Team are working on transporting their equipment from their old storage shed to a new storage container that is more suited for the needs of Team Koalafied’s needs. Meanwhile, the Software Team is working on calibrating the mechanism related to motor power and ball ejection speed. The Software Team is also moving forward with ‘vision code’ that will help identify the target it will be shooting at, following identification, the robot will then adjust the distance and angle it is at in relation to the identified target.


21st October 2018

T - 11 Weeks

Today the team has been up and running on tasks. The Programming team have started configuring the onboard electronic system for the robot, a long with adding safety features to it. These include the lights and circuit breakers. The build team have been testing and adjusting the basketball shooting robot, changes were discussed and an outdoor test was conducted. Lastly preparations were made in turn for the upcoming Team Koalafied Open Day from the Media Team. They have been working on printing, laminating and cutting up the posters for Open Day, plus the brochures that are to be handed out on the day. The website has also been worked on with the design aspects of changing formats and colours.


7th October 2018

T - 13 Weeks

Today the team is preparing for our upcoming Babcock visit that will be very exciting. Also, the Mechanical team is updating the inventory for the upcoming season’s robot parts. The plans for the upcoming quiz night are being finalized. Team Koalafied is now selling squares on our “Friends of Team Koalafied” banner. This is an opportunity for parents, grandparents and friends to show their support for Koalafied and have your square on view throughout 2018/19. You can use this square for your company logo, family crest or personal photograph! The electronic version will be on our website, and the physical banner will come with us wherever we go in 2019. There are 36 squares available, and they are already selling fast. You can purchase your own square here. Make sure to act fast to get the square that you want, because once someone has bought a square, you can’t!


30 September 2018

T - 14 Weeks

Today the team had some interesting work. The media team is working on a large variety of things such as: our upcoming quiz night, our upcoming open day, our visit to Babcock and the south pacific and southern cross-regional s strategy. But the media team aren’t the only people doing work, the build team is either working on strategy or working on making our robot able to shoot basketballs!


16 September 2018

T - 16 Weeks

Today there are quite a lot of different things going on. The media team is planning and preparing our upcoming quiz night and our open day. The build team is busy producing the basketball shooting mechanism. The mechanism itself is split into three main parts to make: The drive base that they were were learning about and working hard on and is the core of making it all work, the ramp and the ball shooter. Lastly, the software team is working on creating the code so that the robot can detect the objects at different distances and also coding for the LED lights.


2 September 2018

T - 18 Weeks

This week we had a member from the Adelaide university to talk to the mechanical and the software team about how they entered an Amazon robotic competition to select, pick and place objects from shelves into bins and they won, competing against teams from around the globe including MIT. He also showed us a video showing how their software can analyse a view from a car camera and identify all the objects in the video – pedestrians, other vehicles, the curb, the road, buildings etc. Once he left some mentors showed the students a prototype for the basketball shooter. this week the media team is doing a huge array of different jobs that have been dealt out to a mentor and a student to work on such as writing letters to sponsors and planning things such as our open day and our quiz night.


26 August 2018

T - 19 Weeks

This week the software mentors gave some cool sessions to the students that are interested in pursuing coding. Most of the students in management and mechanical also decided to learn more about software so they went to the sessions as well. Meanwhile the media team had some of the students making some adjustments to the old website. and the mentors had a meeting to discuss some ideas for fundraising.
